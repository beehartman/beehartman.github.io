    Our Mission

    Pony Panic & Co. focuses on creating high-quality stickers and electronic cases that combine the playful aesthetics of 1980s-inspired ponies with iconic horror film imagery, offering unique and collectible designs for fans of both genres.

    Our mission is to create unique, high-quality stickers and electronic cases inspired by 1980s My Little Pony characters, reimagining classic horror icons as cute, collectible mascots. We offer a fun, nostalgic twist for fans seeking playful non-traditional horror merchandise.
